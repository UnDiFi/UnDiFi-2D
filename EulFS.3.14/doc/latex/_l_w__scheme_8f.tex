\begin{DoxyParagraph}{Purpose}

Computes the U\+CV scheme for scalar problems on a triangle/tetrahedron.

The U\+CV scheme is attributed to \href{http://arc.aiaa.org/doi/abs/10.2514/6.1990-104}{\texttt{ Giles et al. Upwind control volumes -\/ A new upwind approach, A\+I\+AA 90-\/0104}}

Its Fluctuation Splitting version is attributed to Paillere and described in \href{http://dx.doi.org/doi:10.1016/j.jcp.2004.11.023}{\texttt{ De Palma et al. Journal of Computational Physics 208 (2005) 1â€“3}}

The U\+CV scheme is in fact the Lax Wendroff scheme with a particular choice of the elemental time step; the distribution weights are\+:

\[ \beta_i^{UCV} = \left( \frac{1}{d+1} + \frac{2}{3} \frac{ k_{i} }{ \sum_{\ell=1}^{d+1} |k_{\ell}| } \right) = \left( \frac{1}{d+1} + \frac{2}{3} \frac{ k_{i} }{ \sum_{\ell=1}^{d+1} \left( k_{\ell}^{+} - k_{\ell}^{-} \right) } \right) \]
