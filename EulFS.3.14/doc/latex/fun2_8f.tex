Source term for the convection equation \[ u_t + \lambda_x u_x+\lambda_y u_y = f \]

the source term is\-: \[ f = -\left(x\cos\delta+x\sin\delta\right) \] and the exact steady solution is\-: \[ u = \sin\left(2\pi\left(y\cos\delta-x\sin\delta\right)\right)+ x'y' -xy \] where \begin{eqnarray*} x' &=& x - \cos(\delta) (x\cos(\delta)+y\sin(\delta)) \\ y' &=& y - \sin(\delta) (x\cos(\delta)+y\sin(\delta)) \end{eqnarray*}
