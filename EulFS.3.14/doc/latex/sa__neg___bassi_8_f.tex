{\bfseries{S\+A\+\_\+\+N\+A\+G\+\_\+\+B\+A\+S\+SI}} 

\begin{DoxyParagraph}{Purpose}

\end{DoxyParagraph}
This routine discretizes the SA (standard) version of the Spalart-\/\+Allmaras turbulence model transport equation for the K\+I\+N\+E\+M\+A\+T\+IC viscosity to be used in the coupled solution approach for compressible flows

\[ \frac{D\tilde{\nu}}{Dt} = c_{b_1} \left[ 1 - f_{t_2} \right] \tilde{S} \tilde{\nu} - \frac{1}{Re} \left[ c_{w_1} f_{w} - \frac{c_{b_1}}{\kappa^2} f_{t_2} \right] \left[ \frac{\tilde{\nu}}{d} \right]^2 + \frac{1}{\sigma Re} \left[ \nabla \cdot \left( \left( \nu + \tilde{\nu} \right) \nabla \tilde{\nu} \right) + c_{b_2} \left( \nabla \tilde{\nu} \right)^2 \right] \]

Nondimensionalization also leads to the redefinition of the $ \tilde{S} $ and $r$ variables\+: \[ \tilde{S} \equiv \frac{U_{0}}{L_{0}} \left( \Omega + \frac{1}{Re_{0}} \frac{\tilde{\nu} f_{v2}} {\kappa^2 d^2 } \right) \] \[ r \equiv \frac{1}{Re_{0}} \, \frac{\tilde{\nu}}{\tilde{S} \kappa^2 d^2 } \]

As far as the limiting of $ \tilde{S} $ is concerned, we use\+: \[ \tilde{S} = \max \left( \tilde{S}, 0.3\,\Omega \right) \]

which is referred to as \char`\"{}limiting (b)\char`\"{} in \href{http://turbmodels.larc.nasa.gov/spalart.html}{\texttt{ http\+://turbmodels.\+larc.\+nasa.\+gov/spalart.\+html}}
